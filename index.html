What is this quiz for?

This quiz is designed to help you explore how your memory tends to work — whether it is more focused on individual detail, emotional experience, and vivid scenes, or more about relationships, context, and structure.

By answering a few quick questions, you'll get a clearer sense of your memory orientation and how culture, attention, and personal meaning shape what you remember.
